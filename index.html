How Scoring Works

The whole engine runs on the official draw pots — the underdog currency. Picking favorites to do favorite things scores the floor; calling an upset and nailing it pays off big.

Group stage — points for each team = 2 + 2 × |Pot − Finish position|. A Pot 1 team winning its group is chalk (2 pts). A Pot 4 team you call to win its group is worth 8.

Knockout — every team you keep alive in a round banks Round Base × Pot. The deeper a low-pot team runs, the more it prints. Points bank per round, so a Cinderella survivor pays every step.

Tiebreaker — if two brackets tie on points, it goes to whoever picked the champion; still tied, it's decided by the most perfectly-called groups (all four spots correct).
